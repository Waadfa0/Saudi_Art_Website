Saudi Fine Art

The history of Saudi plastic art
began at the beginning of time
in 1928, and since ancient times, the Arabs
have been known for creating various
artistic sculptures, expressive paintings,
and creatively woven embroideries, which
told the story of history and civilization.

Types of international and Saudi fine art: drawing and coloring,Sculptures,Photography,Mosaic,Writing in Arabic script,Collage,Architectural Design and Design by Illustrator.




Art has always developed with the development of the technological platforms provided to it. Many modern artists have moved away from traditional media, such as: drawing with charcoal, oil, acrylic, and watercolor, and are focusing on contemporary fine art using various modern computer programs such as: Photoshop, Illustrator, Tool Paint Sai, and Motion Video Maker.

The most important schools of fine art:
The artistic rules depend on the schools that the artist studies and whose rules the artist applies. The rules of the different schools of fine art form the boundaries that the artist may adhere to in order to completely reverse them, or break them to create a new artistic school and rules that are completely different from the previous one. In this way, art has developed throughout the ages.
